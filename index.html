Get to Know Me

About Me

I am a Full Professor at the Department of Mathematics, Faculty of Science, Naresuan University, Thailand, where I have been advancing mathematical research and education since 2002.

My research bridges advanced mathematics with cutting-edge artificial intelligence, focusing on fixed point theory, machine learning algorithms, and optimization methods. I specialize in developing novel optimizers and deep learning architectures grounded in rigorous mathematical foundations.

My work translates theoretical advances into real-world AI applications, particularly in medical imaging AI (automated diagnosis, glaucoma detection, pneumonia classification) and energy AI (smart grid optimization, renewable energy systems). I am passionate about creating algorithms that not only push the boundaries of mathematical understanding but also solve critical challenges in healthcare and sustainable energy.

As Director of the Research Center for Academic Excellence in Mathematics at Naresuan University, I lead interdisciplinary research initiatives and mentor the next generation of researchers at the intersection of mathematics, machine learning, and AI applications.
